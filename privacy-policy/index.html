Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

AgencyLister is a free marketing agency directory owned and operated by Landing Page Labs, based in Melbourne, Australia. The site helps people find marketing agencies by category and country. That's it.

What data we collect

We collect very little. If you browse the directory, we don't collect any personal information from you.

The only time we collect data is if you submit your agency through the "List your agency" form. In that case, we collect:

  • Agency name
  • Website URL
  • Description
  • Category
  • Country

This information is used solely to create your listing on the directory.

How we use your data

If you submit an agency listing, we use the information you provide to display your agency on AgencyLister. We don't sell, rent, or share your data with anyone for marketing purposes.

Cookies and tracking

We don't use cookies for tracking. We don't run analytics tools on this site. No third-party tracking scripts are loaded when you visit AgencyLister.

Third-party services

We use BunnyCDN (bunny.net) to serve images on the site. BunnyCDN may process standard server logs (like IP addresses) as part of delivering content. You can review their privacy policy at bunny.net/privacy.

We don't use any other third-party services that process visitor data.

Data retention

Agency listing data is kept for as long as the listing is live on the site. If you want your listing removed, get in touch and we'll take care of it.

Your rights

If you've submitted a listing and want it updated or removed, just reach out. We're happy to help.

Contact

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle data, we'll update this page. Given the simplicity of this site, we don't expect frequent changes.
